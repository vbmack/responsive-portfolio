About Me

Now here's a little story I've got to tell
About one humble professional you don't know so well
It started 22 years ago in the Q.C.
With teaching, pre-college, and outreach at UNCC

I had a small start as a high school teacher
Stayed in the classroom for two years with math as the feature
Got featured in the Observer and UNCC started calling
They said STEM-enrichment, and I was all in

For ten years I led Summer Scholars and Saturday Academies
Science fairs, science olympiad, and internships if you please
The Pre-College Program helped many kids get in to college
I grew with the kids and increased my knowledge

In 2006, I transitioned to Educational Outreach
Community engagement for the College of Education was now in my reach
Conferences, workshops, competitions, and collaboration
Made work, not work, but a celebration

But as a life-long learner, new challenges are on the horizon
Web development and coding have me trying
To explore a new dimension, full of wonder
Ben Franklin had lightning, coding shall be my thunder

Thank you for indulging in my rhyme above
I hope you found it entertaining, I understand that you may not love
But if you have opportunities for me to explore my creativity
